Favorite Animes according to Lucas

My top 3 animes of all times.

You can click the links to the myanimelist pages if you interested to know more about the animes.

Mujin Wakusei Survive

One of my childhood favorites that tell the importance of cooperation and friendship for survival.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

A remaster of another childhood anime that has amazing teaching on the price/consequences of actions.

Plastic Memories

Really touching romance that goes deep into the shortness of life and how important is the journey while it lasts.
